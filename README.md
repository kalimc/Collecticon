# Collecticon

Privacy Policy — Collecticon

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Collecticon does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All photos, items, folders, and other content you create in the app are stored locally on your device.

We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party services. We do not have access to your data.

When you use the "Track Package" feature, tapping the tracking link opens the carrier's website in Safari. We do not send any data to those carriers — you are simply redirected to their public tracking page.

If you export a backup, the file is created locally and shared only through methods you choose (AirDrop, Files, etc.).
